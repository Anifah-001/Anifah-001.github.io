My Projects

Comparative Analysis of the Impact of Agricultural Value and Oil Rent on the Nigerian Economy

A data-driven analysis of Nigeria's economic performance (2000–2021), comparing the contributions of Agricultural GDP and Oil Revenue to economic growth. The project utilized Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI for data cleaning, analysis, visualization, and dashboard development. Key findings revealed that Agriculture contributed more consistently and sustainably to economic growth, while Oil Revenue exhibited greater volatility due to fluctuations in global oil market conditions, highlighting the need for economic diversification.

Investigating Factors Influencing Academic Performance Change (Decline or Improvement): A Learning Analytics Approach

A learning analytics project that examined the factors influencing changes in academic performance among 191 undergraduate students from two different universities. The project utilized Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI for data collection, analysis, visualization, and dashboard development. Key findings revealed that study habits, sleep duration, financial stress, social media usage, and motivation significantly influenced academic performance, with motivated students and those maintaining balanced study and sleep schedules showing greater academic improvement. The study provides data-driven recommendations for supporting at-risk students and enhancing academic success.
